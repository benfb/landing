Defcon One

Defcon One is a site I built to help people who struggle with psychological disorders to send messages and get in touch with friends, family, and hotlines through the touch of a button. It is my first major web project, built with node.js, express, and Bootstrap.

I released a mobile version of the site, and an iPhone app version as well.

Sequences

Sequences is a ruby gem I made when I was learning about geometric and arithmetic sequences in Algebra II. It's incredibly simple, but I did actually publish it to the ruby gems repository. It calculates terms or sums of different mathematical series.
